OVERVIEW

Meridian School is one of the finest schools in the twin cities, with a strong intellectual pulse with a meaningful motto, ‘Connecting the mind and heart.’

Keeping pace with today’s vibrant and competitive environment, the Best School in Banjara Hills, Meridian School, understands the importance of cooperative teaching and its positive influence on children. This enables us to engage students in experiential learning and nurture innovators, educators, leaders and learners of 21st century. Academics has always been the top priority of the school. It has the credit of a record of cent percent pass results till date. It also produced National and State level rank holders.

Abhinaya Otikunta secured 1st rank in Telangana State in AISSE 2019-20

Harshini Maddala secured 3rd rank at All India Level in AISSE 2018-19

Apart from the consistent success rate at the Boards, the school enrolls its students in national and international competitive examinations like SOF, NELTAS, IAIS, NOF, Talent Search exams etc. wherein many students have bagged excellent results and certificates of extraordinary performance and top rankers.

ABOUT US

Meridian is a New Generation Educational Group committed to educate children for life and thus bring a change in the society. Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.

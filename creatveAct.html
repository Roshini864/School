CREATIVE ACTIVITIES

Being creative helps you become a better problem solver. Creative people are better abled to live with uncertainty. Meridian provides the right platform to hone the creative skills among the students. Performing Arts play a significant role in the co-curricular activities of the school. State of art infrastructure gives the right impetus to develop creative skills. Students are given various opportunities through regular assemblies, special celebrations, competitions etc. Our school Annual Days are always the best productions in the twin cities. Some of our creative initiatives are:

Under The Mango Tree – Reading Club

International Dimension in Schools – A British Council Programme

Confero – Debating Club

Melange – Interschool Literary and Cultural Fest

Music Club – In association with Furtados School of Music

Moot Court, MUNs, Sculpture, Heritage Club, Eco Club, Rotary Interact Club, Filmit, Theatre, Western and Classical Music and Dance, Painting, Photography and many more.

ABOUT US

Meridian is a New Generation Educational Group committed to educate children for life and thus bring a change in the society. Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.

SCOUTS AND GUIDES
The School is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi since its inception in the year 1995 and has evolved into one of the best CBSE Schools in the country. The IB offers a continuum of International Education. This programme encourages both personal and academic achievements, challenging students to excel in their studies and in their personal development The school has been conferred three times consecutively with the International School Award by the British Council. It is now called International Dimension in Schools. This programme fosters International ethos in schools embedding it within the curriculum. To help build a better world where people are self fulfilled as individuals and play a constructive role in society, the school has started Scouts and Guides programme in collaboration with Universal Scouts and Guides.

ABOUT US

Meridian is a New Generation Educational Group committed to educate children for life and thus bring a change in the society. Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.

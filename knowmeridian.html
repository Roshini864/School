KNOW MERIDIAN

Meridian Educational Society has completed a quarter of a century and is poised for a quantum leap into the future by adapting to the challenges of an ever-evolving educational scenario. Connecting the Mind and Heart fluidly and flawlessly, it creates a holistic new millennial global generation of citizens who are high achievers, balancing professional commitment with a social conscience.


Meridian School for Boys and Girls, Banjara Hills was founded on 12th July, 1995, by Mr. Neelakanta and Mrs. Renuka Butta with a vision to offer quality education. The Flagship School has grown from strength to strength and has achieved unparalleled success not only in academics but also in co-scholastic activities like performing arts and sports ensuring 360o achievement. As the school celebrates its Silver Jubilee Year in 2020, the success of the school is validated by its Alumni many of whom have gone on to study in prestigious institutes like IITs, NITs, AFMC, JIPMER and global premium institutes like the IVY Leagues. The success of the flagship school led to the founding of equally successful branches at Madhapur in 2006, Kukatpally in 2008 and most recently at Uppal in 2020.

ABOUT US

Meridian is a New Generation Educational Group committed to educate children for life and thus bring a change in the society. Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.

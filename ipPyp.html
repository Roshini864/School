IB PYP COORDINATOR

Ms. Sailaja Koduri

Recipient of the Dr. S. Radhakrishnan Aadarsh Shikshak Samman Award , Sailaja Koduri is the IB PYPC with 20 + years of teaching experience. Her passion for teaching made her leave a lucrative job in the Corporate world. Armed with MA, PGDBM, PGCTE AND PGDTE she is a qualified Primary Years Programme Coordinator and Workshop Leader prior to which she has been teaching English Literature .

A silver medallist in HR she has been a workshop leader for the SAIBSA South Asia IB Schools Association Job Alike Sessions in South India as well. An avid reader, artist and a perfectionist Sailaja has brought IB PYP to Meridian School, Banjara Hills within 2 years of the candidacy phase. She has been the Programme of Inquiry Developer and Curriculum Coordinator since the inception of IB PYP. She has undergone intense training and attended many workshops and was the Single Point of Contact for the TCS Study Program and won the Fastest Learner Award.

Approachable and amicable Sailaja goes by the motto ‘ All Achievers are Believers and All Believers are Achievers’ and strongly believes that meticulous dedicated work coupled with passion for the job are the stepping stones to success. She sees to it that the learnings and teachings percolate down to both the teacher and student / parent level believing that all three are pillars for the Education Industry. She is a strong advocator of participative and collaborative teaching and learning at all levels.

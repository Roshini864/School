GLOBAL PARTNERSHIP COORDINATOR

Ms. Rajatha Gora

Ms.Rajatha ,a Certified Career Counsellor is a multi talented Senior Secondary teacher of Mass Media and has been with Meridian School for over one and a half decade in various capacities. Apart from holding a Masters degree in English Literature, Ms. Rajatha received Cambridge Teacher’s Training and had participated in Intel Teach Programme. She was one among the final nominees for the 2019 International Career and College Counselling , IC3 Awards for her accomplishment as a successful career counsellor.

She is a passionate dancer and being a very active youth, won many awards in her college days like Best Youth Award, Nikhil Koithara Award for Young activists, First Female and Youngest District Rotaract Representative and many more. Rajatha has lead and been part of International Youth Exchange programmes. Compassionate at heart, Ms.Rajatha involves herself in the activities of many Social Service Organisations. She possesses excellent collaborative and interpersonal skills

As the Career Guidance Counsellor at Meridian, Ms.Rajatha is in-charge of organizing activities, workshops, field trips, exposure visits, guest lectures and internships for students from grade I – XII. She is also a Faculty advisor for Meridian Model United Nations sessions. She also plans and executes International exposure programmes for students and Internships at CERN, WFUNA, FUTURESAKE and International MUNs. With her empathetic approach to the teenagers , She has guided many aspirants to their dream courses and careers through her counselling.

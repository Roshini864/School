PRINCIPAL

William Butler Yeats rightly said, ‘Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire.’ We at Meridian are deeply committed to kindle that spark in the child which would gradually extinguish if not guarded. We are here to inspire and empower young minds to think independently, integrate, excel and contribute to the betterment of the society. Anyone who walks through the gates of Meridian , be it a student, a teacher or a parent feels welcomed and cared for. Such is the warmth that one experiences at Meridian.

As I took the charge of the post of Principal of Meridian Banjara Hills, I could sense the warmth and love of the Meridian family. I too felt welcomed with open hands. Going by the very motto of the school, I would try my best to connect the mind and heart in every fresh and innovative step that I would take in connection with administration. Keeping in view the online instruction for the past one and half years, we have introduced the concept of a bridge course as the students moved to the next grade to drill them with the main concepts before the serious teaching started. Catering to the demands of the NEP, we are also revising and reviewing our teaching strategies and instruction tools at all levels. We are proud to claim the credit of possessing the best teaching faculty in the twin cities with excellent qualification, years of experience and unflinching commitment towards their noble profession. We are here not only to impart holistic education complete with 21st Century skills and moral values, but also to develop in the students, the ability to apply, analyse, organise and think out of the box and to connect everything they learn to real life.

We invite parents, as stake holders of this institution, to take an active role in the grooming of the child and help us do our best and their valuable suggestions will always be received in good spirit.

Academics is our main objective but not cramming. Freedom is given but not at the cost of discipline. Here is our promise to you: In our preoccupation with the process of assessment of scholastic and co scholastic areas, we will not forget our primary goal of anchoring our dear Meridianites in a sound value system.

Wishing Meridian All the Best for all times.

Nishi Rana

Principal

ABOUT US

Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.

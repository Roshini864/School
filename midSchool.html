MIDDLE SCHOOL INCHARGE

Ms. Padmashree Madabhushi

Mrs.Padmashree Madabhushi , In-Charge Middle School, has had a long and enriching association with Meridian Banjara both as a parent and as a teacher of Legal Studies at the Senior Secondary level. Recipient of the Telangana State Best Teacher Award from Telangana Government , she combines her administrative abilities with academic initiatives to bring Quality Learning. Her contribution to the success of the school as In-Charge Primary and now as In-Charge Middle School remains unparalleled. Actively involved in Social Outreach Programs like flood relief measures she inculcates a sense of social commitment in students. She combines passion with perseverance in the pursuit of excellence and leads through example and inspiration.

As a Legal Studies instructor, Ms.Padmashree is instrumental in initiating the Inter school Moot Court competition the first ever of its kind in the twin cities. She also handles the responsibility of organizing various Inter school competitions and orientation programmes like Scouts and Guides which enabled the students to join an interactive session with the President of India, His Excellency Ramnath Kovind. Her role in the working of the school is valuable and indispensable.

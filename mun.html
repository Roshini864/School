Meridian MUN 2020

Meridian School Banjara Hills hosted its Fifth Edition of Model United Nations, Meridian MUN 2020 on 30th & 31st October 2020. But this time with a difference ! Keeping the current situation in mind the sessions were organised on a virtual platform but without losing its originality and gravity.

Along with the two-day conference with extremely talented and proficient Executive Boards, ROP (Rules of Procedure) Training Sessions for the delegates were organised, and a Career fair by some of the top universities in the world to provide exposure to the delegates and equip them with the necessary skill sets they may need during the conference and their future endeavours as well.

CAREER COUNSELLING & GUIDANCE

Career guidance at MERIDIAN begins in Class 9.

The school hosts University Fairs, wherein groups of Universities from USA, Canada, UK, Australia and India visit the campus each year to provide information about the courses on offer.

The school hosts University Fairs, wherein groups of Universities from USA, Canada, UK, Australia and India visit the campus each year to provide information about the courses on offer.

Guest Lectures and Professional Career Advice

Choosing a career or program of study is a daunting task. Meridian provides our students with opportunities to meet experts from different fields who can offer a wealth of experience to allow students to make educated decisions for their futures.

Essay Writing Workshops

While grades and test scores are important, an exceptional college application essay is a crucial factor in University Application process.

To help students learn much of what is needed to write that standout application essay. This year also we organized Essay Writing Workshops that help students get started and work their way through the essay writing process.

Preparatory courses and testing for SAT, PSAT, AP and ACT

We guide students related to Preparatory courses and tests like SAT, PSAT, AP and ACT

Personalised Attention

One-on-one counselling is offered to students to know their aptitudes, skills and preferences, to help them identify Universities, majors and courses best suited for their individual priorities and needs.

Summer Internship

Meridian’s career guidance department provides students with support to participate in virtual internships and summer internships at reputed organizations across different industries. Our students have participated and won accolades at the Harvard, Oxford and other University summer programmes.

Alumni Interactions

Alumni sessions are organized to increase interaction and knowledge sharing.

Meridian alumni are attending the best Universities in India and around the world.

ABOUT US

Meridian is a New Generation Educational Group committed to educate children for life and thus bring a change in the society. Learning philosophy practised at Meridian infuses traditional values into a progressive approach, equipping students to adapt and excel anywhere in the world. Our alumni, working in diverse fields, have found Meridian to be the best school for a successful career take off.
